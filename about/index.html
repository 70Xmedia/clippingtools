About Us

We test clipping tools so you don't have to guess

ClippingTools is an independent review site dedicated to one job: helping creators, streamers, and marketers find the right clipping tool for their workflow, without the guesswork.

Why we started ClippingTools

Short-form video exploded, and so did the number of "AI clipping tools" promising to turn your long videos into viral shorts overnight. Some of them are genuinely excellent. Others are recycled templates with a marketing page bolted on top. It became genuinely difficult to tell the difference from the outside — every landing page uses the same words: "AI-powered," "viral," "one-click."

We built ClippingTools to cut through that noise. We group tools by how you'll actually use them — online and browser-based, offline and desktop-first, or gaming-specific — and we review each one against the same criteria: clip quality, real-world pricing at volume, workflow fit, and independent evidence beyond the vendor's own marketing.

What we look for in a good clipping tool

  • Genuine moment detection. Does the AI find the clip a human editor would pick, or just slice the video into even chunks?
  • Honest pricing. We look at cost per minute or per video at realistic volume, not just the cheapest headline number.
  • The right fit for the job. A podcaster, a Twitch streamer, and a marketing agency need different things from a clipping tool, and we review with that context in mind.
  • Evidence beyond the sales page. We weigh vendor claims against independent reviews, community feedback, and app-store ratings.
