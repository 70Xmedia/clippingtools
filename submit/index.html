Tool submission form

Submitting opens your email client addressed to support@clippingapps.com with your details. We review every submission — no fee required to be considered.

What happens next

  1. We review your submission and, where possible, test the tool ourselves.
  2. If it meets our bar for quality and usefulness, we add an honest review to the relevant category.
  3. We'll email you directly if we need more information or once your review is published.

Please note

Submitting your tool doesn't guarantee inclusion or a specific rating. Our reviews stay independent — see our Disclaimer for details on how we evaluate submissions and handle any affiliate relationships.
